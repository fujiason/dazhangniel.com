In The Heights But It's Asian

This was a project I led in 2021 as a parody of the opening song to "In The Heights" by Lin Manuel Miranda. It has recieved critical acclaim and has been noticed by director John M. Chu.

Wuxing Warriors

AAA's biggest end-of-the-year event is back! Fashion Show is an annual dance showcase held by UIUC's Asian American Association. We hold weekly practices where students of all dance experiences can learn a variety of popular and pan-Asian dance styles.

Fashion Show Trailer 2023

This short film first premiered as part of the UIUC AAA 36th Annual Fashion Show, a dance showcase of Asian American culture and talent. The film was played alongside live performances, hence why the film is split into parts. Together, the film and the performances tell a cohesive story. I highly recommend watching Wuxing Warriors along with the dances to get the full experience! You can find the full cut of Fashion Show on the official AAA YouTube channel: https://youtu.be/JaKZmpLrpaQ
