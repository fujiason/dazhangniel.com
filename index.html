CINEMATOGRAPHER, PROGRAMMER, AND ARTIST

About Daniel

Daniel is a cinematographer, programmer, and artist based in Chicago, Illinois. He is a second-generation Chinese American and has worked on multiple independent films, such as the highly appraised "In The Heights But It's Asian" and "Wuxing Warriors". With over 600,000 views on his main YouTube channel, he hopes to inspire others and convey the message of cultural identity and appreciation for the arts.
