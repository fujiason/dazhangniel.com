Daniel Zhang's Highlight Reel Fall 2022


Thanks for watching my content! I'm a videographer and emerging filmmaker based in Chicago, Illinois. For the last eight years, I've been perfecting my creative style through independent short films and digital media. I've lent my unique style and technical ability to produce and edit high quality dance videos, promotional videos, wedding videos, commercials, infomercials, music videos, and more.

In The Heights

×

In The Heights


Directing my friends in this project was an incredible experience. Although we were recklessly inspired fresh off of watching the movie, we sought out ways that we could really make the idea work. In The Heights is indisputably a story about Latino immigrants in the US, so rewriting the lyrics and shifting the narrative was an important step to respecting the source material and recognizing an opportunity for representation.

AAA 36th Fashion Show

×

AAA 36th Fashion Show


For the past 36 years, the Asian American Association (AAA) has hosted Fashion Show on the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign campus. While it originally began as a traditional fashion show, it eventually evolved into the dance showcase that it is known for today. Fashion Show, AAA's largest event of the year, showcases both culture and talent through choreography, digital media, and more. This year's Fashion Show was made possible with the support of Campus Circle, HERE Champaign, Latea, Octave, and Smile Student Living.
